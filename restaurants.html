Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine

Enjoy rice and fish from the local food chain "Fishes and Rices." Fishes and Rices has been in business for over 100 years and has made a name for itself, establishing 10 different stores across the island. When you visit, they will be sure to provide exceptional food and service for your vacation or business trip!
