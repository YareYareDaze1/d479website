Grocery

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

"Food Supermarket" is a supermarket store that is exclusive to Taniti Island. Food Supermarket has two stores on the island and provides fresh and delicious groceries.

Business Locations:

--6 Road Road
--7 Roady Road

"Gro" Grocery Store is a grocery store that is also exclusive to Taniti Island. Gro has two stores on the island and specializes in providing locals with fresh fruits.

Business Locations:

--4 Road Road
--10 Roady Road

Enjoy quick food from the "Conbini" convenience store. Conbini is open 24 hours a day and offers a wide variety of goods for purchase..

Business Location:

--24 Road Road
